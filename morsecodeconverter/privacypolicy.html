Morse Code Converter by Damian Muca - privacy policy

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Morse Code Converter ("Application") for mobile devices that was created by Damian Muca.

Application is available to download in Google Play (link to Application).

  • Morse Code Converteris is a simple android application that allow you to convert your text into Morse signals in several forms i.e. text, blinking screen, and flash signals.
  • Application does not collect personal information like name, surname, age, place of birth.
  • We use non-personal information to improve Application. We use Google Analytics.
  • Application needs camera permission to convert your text into Morse code. It neither record video nor take photos.
